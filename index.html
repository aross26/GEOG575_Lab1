United States Breweries, 1984 to Now

This map is a visualization of annual brewery counts by state collected by the US Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, and Explosives beginning in 1984 and ending in Q2 2019.
Craft breweries have grown exponentially in recent decades, in some states growing upwards of 1,000% since 2000.

Drag the slider bar below to see how much has changed.
